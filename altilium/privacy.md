<section class="content-section">
  <h2>Data Collection</h2>
  <p><strong>Altilium does not collect, use, store, or transfer any data from your personal device.</strong></p>
  <p>All calculations and data processing happen entirely on-device. Your threshold configurations, results, and any exported PDFs remain on your device and are never uploaded to any external server.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>No Accounts Required</h2>
  <p>Altilium does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no user registration, authentication, or identity tracking of any kind.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>No Analytics or Tracking</h2>
  <p>Altilium does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or third-party tracking tools. We have no visibility into how you use the app.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>Local Storage</h2>
  <p>Any data you enter into Altilium (such as battery voltage, lead impedance, and threshold values) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not synced to any cloud service and remains entirely under your control.</p>
  <p>You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>PDF Export</h2>
  <p>When you export results as a PDF, the file is generated locally on your device. How you choose to share or store that PDF (via email, cloud storage, etc.) is entirely up to you and governed by those respective services' privacy policies.</p>
</section>
