<section class="content-section">
  <h2>Data Collection</h2>
  <p><strong>GoVision does not collect, use, store, or transfer any data from your device.</strong></p>
  <p>All image processing happens entirely on-device using Apple's Core ML framework. Your photos, captured board positions, and SGF files remain on your iPhone and are never uploaded to any server.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>No Accounts Required</h2>
  <p>GoVision does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no user registration, authentication, or identity tracking of any kind.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>No Analytics or Tracking</h2>
  <p>GoVision does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or third-party tracking tools. We have no visibility into how you use the app.</p>
</section>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>Camera & Photo Library Access</h2>
  <p>GoVision requests access to your camera and photo library solely to capture and import images of Go boards for scoring. These images are processed on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.</p>
</section>
